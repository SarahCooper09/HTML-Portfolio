My Top 3

1. 3 Idiots

3 Idiots is an Indian Movie that is based on a group of friends that are filled with humor. These friends are a fond of pranks and having fun.

2. Murder Mystery 2

The title, "Murder Mystery 2" is pretty self-explanitory. This movie is about a couple who meet up with a billionare in a commercial airplane. They end up becoming good friends and are invited to a family event on a an expensive yacht. However, someone from the family is actually a traitor.

3. Lift

Lift is a comedy movie about a heist for $500 million worth of gold.
